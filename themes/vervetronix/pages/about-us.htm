A few things about us

VerveTronix is more about simplicity, mass appeal, crowdsource, open hardware software platforms and lean profits instead of brand-legacy, proprietary technology, and premium price-tags. The result is that we are able to create products that either have no easy alternatives in the market or are priced much lower than any of the alternatives.

We are so obsessed by our quality and consumer experience that we retain maximum possible control of manufacturing right under our noses instead of blindly outsourcing it to outsourcing havens. Yeah, it's harder this way but we don't plan on shying away from hard things just because they're worth it.
