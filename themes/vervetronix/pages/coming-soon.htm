The Mojo is an 8-switch foot-controller that enables you to tap into the power of modern computers/laptops easily, quickly, and without compromising on quality. While being nearly half the price of most decent multi-effects units out there.

  • Easily assign both effects and functions to it’s 8 foot-switches using almost any DAW.
  • Nearly unlimited number of effects and capabilities at your toe-tips.
  • Simple USB connection to your computer.
  • Doesn’t need a separate power supply or battery.
  • Fully Plug-n-Play. Needs no additional driver/software installation.
  • Works with Windows, Mac OS and Linux platforms.
  • Not limited to just guitars or bass, use it for any instrument you can imagine.
  • Stainless Steel Body so it can stand against you and the elements.
  • Does not touch your audio signal at all.
  • Highest quality components.
